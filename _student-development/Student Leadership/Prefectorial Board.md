<p>The 65th Prefectorial Board consists of 53 Prefects who bring together
diverse expertise, allowing them to fulfil the versatile and demanding
role of Prefects effectively. The Board is dedicated to upholding its longstanding
reputation for excellence, a characteristic that has defined it since its
establishment in 1961. Prefects diligently work towards embodying the values
of Christlikeness and serving as exemplars of the school's principles.
They take on responsibilities such as planning and leading a wide range
of activities and events, aiming to maintain the good name of Anglo-Chinese
School (Independent) and positively impact the student body.</p>
<h3>Vision Statement</h3>
<p>Ever-growing united leaders, led by God to serve and inspire.</p>
