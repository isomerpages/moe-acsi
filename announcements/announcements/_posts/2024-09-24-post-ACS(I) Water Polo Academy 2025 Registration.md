<p>The ACS(I) Water Polo Academy (WPA) is a niche youth development programme
dedicated to teaching the fundamentals of Water Polo in a safe, healthy
and fun environment.</p>
<p>The programme is held at ACS(I), run by our teachers and in-house water
polo coaches. It seeks to provide another avenue for boys interested in
water polo to pick up the skillsets necessary to enjoy the sport.</p>
