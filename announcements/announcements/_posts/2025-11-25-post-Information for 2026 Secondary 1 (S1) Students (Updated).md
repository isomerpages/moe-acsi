<p><strong>Incoming 2026 Secondary 1 (S1) students DO NOT need to report to school on release of school posting day.</strong></p>
<p>Instead, registration for all students, including DSA students, will be done <strong><u>ONLINE ONLY</u></strong> from ACS(I)’s website.</p>
<p>As communicated by MOE, the S1 Posting Results will be released on Friday, 19 December 2025. (<a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/secondary/s1-posting/results">Source</a>) &nbsp;</p>
<p>Online registration via <a href="https://www.acsindep.moe.edu.sg/admissions/s1-posting-exercise/">ACS(I)’s website</a> will be available ONLY FROM 1.00 pm on Friday, 19 December 2025. Registration will close at 10.00 am on Monday, 22 December 2025.</p>
<p>Parents with no Internet access may register using the laptop in our front office, which will be available from 8.00 am to 4.00 pm on Friday, 19 December and Monday, 22 December (we are closed on Saturday 22 Dec).</p>
<p>The booklist and information on the textbook and uniform vendors will also be available on our website.</p>
