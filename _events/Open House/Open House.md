<p>The ACS(I) Open House will be held on <strong>Saturday, 13 April 2024</strong>,
from <strong>8.30 am – 12.30 pm</strong>.</p>
<p>&nbsp;During the Open House, visitors will be able to interact with our
students and teachers at the various CCA &amp; Subject booths. We will
also provide details on our academic / talent programmes and student services,
along with comprehensive tours of our campus.</p>
<p>&nbsp;<strong>This will be a walk-in event, with no registration required. </strong>As
there is limited parking in the school, visitors are advised to park their
vehicles at the multi-storey carparks nearby.</p>
