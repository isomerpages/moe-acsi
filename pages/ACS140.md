<p><strong>Celebrating 140 Years of Excellence: ACS140</strong>
</p>
<p>The ACS Family commemorates 140 years of heritage, faith and educational
excellence. Since Bishop William Fitzjames Oldham founded the first Anglo-Chinese
School in 1886, we have remained steadfast in our mission to nurture Scholars,
Officers, and Gentlemen.</p>
<p>Guided by our calling to be Catalysts for Change, For God and Humanity,
we look back with gratitude and forward with purpose.</p>
<p>“<em>In the same way, let your light shine before others,</em>
</p>
<p><em>so that they may see your good works and give glory to your Father who is in heaven.</em>”</p>
<p>– Matthew 5:16</p>
<p>This year’s theme verse reflects our enduring commitment: to raise leaders
who act with integrity, serve with conviction, and shine as a light in
the world. ACS140 is more than a milestone; it is a renewed call to carry
the ACS spirit into the future.</p>
<p><strong>The ACS140 Combined Service</strong>
</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Open House/Dr_Ng_Eng_Hen_Subpage.jpg">
</div>
<p>On 26 February 2026, the ACS family gathered at the Singapore National
Stadium for a combined celebration service ahead of Founder’s Day on 1
March. The stands were a sea of red, blue and gold as approximately 12,000
students, staff, alumni and friends from all seven ACS schools came together
to commemorate the event.</p>
<p>We were honoured to have Dr. Eng Ng Hen, Distinguished Old Boy and Former
Minister for Defence, alongside the ACSOBA and members of the wider ACS
family, in attendance for an afternoon of reflection and thanksgiving.&nbsp;</p>
<p>To commemorate this momentous event, ACSOBA produced an ACS140 commemorative
video which premiered at the celebration. The film brings together the
voices of the ACS family leaders to share their reflections and hopes for
the future. Whether you are a current student, an alumnus, or a friend
of the school, the film captures the heart of our collective journey, honouring
our storied past while looking ahead with purpose to what lies ahead as
“One ACS”.</p>

<p><strong>ACS (Independent) Founder’s Day Service</strong>
</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Open House/140_FD_GOH_Momento.png">

<p>Following the combined service at the National Stadium, ACS (Independent)
also held our school-based Founder’s Day celebration and prize presentation
on 27 February 2026. IB and GCE O-Level Class of 2025 award recipients
were recognised for their excellence at Auditorium 2.</p>
<p>It was a warm personal touch to have Mr Arene Koh, Superintendent South
7 at Ministry of Education, Schools Division grace the occasion as our
Guest of Honour, for he was the Principal who faithfully guided the Class
of 2025 through their years.&nbsp;</p>
<p>This celebration also marks the premiere of “AC Forever”, a music video
written and produced from scratch by our students to commemorate our 140<sup>th</sup> year.
The video offers a glimpse into the vibrancy of student life while marking
this milestone year for the ACS family.</p>

<p><strong>Continuing the Legacy</strong>
</p>
<p>As ACS (Independent) celebrates 140 years, we give thanks for the people
who make our school what it is: alumni, parents and friends. These halls
are more than buildings; they are where friendships are forged, values
are nurtured, and generations of Scholars, Officers, and Gentlemen have
been inspired to grow into their fullest potential.</p>
<p>Today, we have an opportunity to ensure that the ACS spirit continues
to transform the lives of students for years to come.</p>
