<p><strong>Boys’ Brigade Day 2024</strong></p><p>On 16 January 2024, The Boys’ Brigade commemorated its 94th annual BB Day, an event that celebrates the founding of the Boys’ Brigade in Singapore.</p><p>Our Boys rehearsed for the special occasion for countless hours, to provide an immaculate display of discipline, teamwork and steadfastness for the ceremony.</p><p>The highlight of the parade was the march-past, where the coordination, soigné and decorum of our Boys were presented to the whole school as they marched alongside the anthemic melodies played by our BB Bagpipe Band.&nbsp;</p><p>BB Day 2024 was graced by our Guest-of-Honour, Mr Shaun Lim, the Deputy Superintendent of the Singapore Police Force. A former BB Boy of 10 years, Mr Lim gave a profound and passionate speech, encouraging our Boys to continue forging lifelong bonds with each other in the BB, and to serve with Christian manliness.&nbsp;</p><p>We owe the success and achievements of our Company to the hard work of our teachers, officers, and the generations of Boys who have contributed their part to the BB legacy.</p><p>SSG/4 Joel Chang Le Xin</p><p>4.16 Enoch</p><p>&nbsp;</p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/ACSpression/2024/Picture24.jpg"></div><p>Mr Lim with the BB Admin of 2023-2024</p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/ACSpression/2024/Picture25.jpg"></div><p>One United Company</p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/ACSpression/2024/Picture26.jpg"></div><p>The March-Past</p>
