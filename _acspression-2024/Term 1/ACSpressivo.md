<p>A decade of artistic passion reached its crescendo on October 27 as ACSpressivo X illuminated ACS(I). CPA2 reverberated with the infectious energy of pop classics as the Music Elective Programme students, IB HL students, and performers from Queensway Secondary School showcased their talents. Meanwhile, the Art Centre walls blossomed with a vibrant display of paintings, sculptures, and digital creations by IB HL and year 1 and 2 Art students.
