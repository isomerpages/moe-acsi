prior to the A &amp; B Division National School Games (NSG) (In Term 2),

to review the training schedule to ensure the school team players are well
prepared for the NSG. Students will be notified of any changes to the schedule
beforehand and they will revert to the fixed training schedule once the
season is over.&nbsp;</p>
