The ACS(I) Bowling team has an illustrious history and prides itself on the good character of our bowlers, our team spirit, and stellar achievements. We believe in giving our best and being worthy ambassadors of the school.

Our team has done exceedingly well in recent editions of the National School Games. Since 2018, we have won at least 2 division championship titles per year. In 2025, we were crowned A and C Division boys champions. We give all glory to God and firmly believe that The Best Is Yet To Be!

**Schedule**

Trainings take place on Monday, Wednesday and Thursday from 4-6pm at Superbowl Mount Faber.
