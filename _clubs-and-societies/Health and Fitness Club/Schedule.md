<p>Wednesday(s)&nbsp; 3:15 pm – 4:40 pm Old MPH for Fitness (Monthly/Bi-Monthly sessions)&nbsp;</p>
<p>Activities: Kick-Boxing, Cross Fit to name a few. Attendance is mandatory&nbsp;</p>
<p>The club meets throughout the year on an ad hoc basis on Wednesdays at MPH 1 from 3.30 pm to 4.30 pm, for approximately 8 sessions throughout the year. As such attendance is compulsory for the first 2 sessions with 1 optional for Year 6 members at the start of the year and for Year 5 members it is compulsory for 5 sessions. In addition, the club occasionally embarks on a Values-In-Action (VIA) project during the June holidays. It is strongly recommended that the Year 5 members participate in the VIA project.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
