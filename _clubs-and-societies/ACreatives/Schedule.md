Training is held every Monday from 3:15pm to 5:15pm at Art Room 2, covering diverse art techniques and styles.

All members are expected to take ownership of their learning by actively engaging in self-directed study of art appreciation and visual literacy. This will be conducted at least twice each term.

There will be additional training sessions held on Wednesday from 3:30pm to 5:30pm during competition period..
