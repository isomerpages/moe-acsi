<p>Check out our collection of award-winning photographs, masterfully captured
by our talented Photog members! These stunning images have garnered prestigious
accolades, such as the “Best Photo Story” award in the Our Schools, Our
Stories competition under the Youth Category's 'My School Community' theme.
Some have even graced the prestigious Ngee Ann Photography Exhibition 2024,
standing as a testament to the creativity, skill, and dedication of our
CCA.</p>
<p>Be sure to witness our Photog members in action as they breathe life into
their creative visions! From capturing everyday moments of school life
to unveiling the wonders that surround us, our Photog members transform
fleeting instants into timeless memories.</p>
<p>By becoming a part of this journey, you, too, can unlock your creative
potential, participate in a wide variety of competitions, and take home
awards that recognize your vision!</p>
<p>Caleb Ong Jia Le (2.10, 2024)</p>
