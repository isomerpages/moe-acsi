**Expectations**
----------------

Students are expected to fulfil two components as members of the Interact Club. They need to ensure **a minimum of 75% attendance in both the service AND club meetings**. Sessions are clearly denoted in the following tables. Outstanding awards will be given to students who are exemplary in their attendance.

**Service Component**

**Total service sessions:** 37 sessions over two years

**Minimum sessions required:** 29 sessions over two years

**Club Meetings Component**

**Club Meetings (Week B, Thursdays, 16:00-17:00)**

Club Meetings are critical because they complement the service component. It is in these meetings that you will meet with your peers and share your learnings and issues. There will be training given during club meetings as well. Interactors are expected to come for at least 75% of these club meetings.

**All club meetings are held on Week B, Thursdays from 16.00-17.00**

Compulsory events that are counted under club meetings for all Year 5s and Y6s are as follows:

1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; **Interact Camp** is held in early April

2.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; **Interact Club Installation (Incoming Exco)** held usually in July

The following are the only reasons accepted as VR which is not part of your 25% allowance

1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Competitions

2.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; MC

3.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Green form

4.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Other reasons approved by the Teachers-in-charge

All VRs or MCs need to be submitted to the IAs within 3 days, failing which, it will be regarded as an absence.
