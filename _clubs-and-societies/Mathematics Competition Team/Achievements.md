<p><strong>2025</strong>
</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>In the <strong>United Kingdom Intermediate Mathematical Challenge (UKIMC)</strong>,
25 students achieved Gold Awards.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>At the <strong>Trinity School Mathematics Contest Winter 2025 Virtual Competition</strong>,
6 teams of 4 students each participated in the Middle and High School divisions.
The team was placed 5<sup>th</sup> out of 75 High School teams worldwide.
They went on to participate in <strong>the Spring 2025 Virtual competition</strong> and
was placed 1<sup>st</sup> out of 21 high school teams participating worldwide.</p>

</li>

medals, and 22 honourable mentions. Notably, Wang Yan Chuan, Jude (1.09
James) secured a gold medal and ranked 29th in the SMO Junior Competition.
Wang Yan Li, Luke (3.11 Elisha) won a gold medal and finished 19th in the
SMO Senior, as well as obtaining another gold and placing 26th in the SMO
Open Competition. Max Richard Khew (1.02 Mark) was awarded gold medals
in both the SMO Junior and SMO Open competitions.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>At the <strong>Singapore Mathematics Project Festival (SMPF)</strong>,
Zachary Nicholas Tan Teon Mo (3.01 Jeremiah) and Wang Yan Li, Luke (3.11
Elisha) won a Bronze prize for their project on Modular Patterns in Pascal’s
Triangle.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>At the<strong> Singapore Mathematics Society Essay Competition</strong>,
Yoon Sae Young (6.01 Antioch) and Zhang Yu Xuan, Matthew (6.07 Ephesus)
won the 1<sup>st</sup> prize in Category C for their essay “By Applying

</li>
<li>
