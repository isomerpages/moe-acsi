**2026 APPLICATION FOR ADMISSION INTO YEAR 3 INTEGRATED PROGRAMME (IP) FOR ACADEMIC YEAR (AY) 2027 FOR STUDENTS FROM OTHER SCHOOLS**


ACS (Independent)’s Year 3 (Secondary 3) Integrated Programme (IP) application exercise for Academic Year 2027 (AY2027) is open for application by current Secondary 2 students in the Secondary Education Certificate (SEC) programme.  
  
Eligibility to apply

  
•  All applicants must fulfil the age requirement for admission into Year 3 for AY2027.  
•  All applicants must not be admitted to their current school via DSA-Sec and are not in the Integrated Programme (IP).  
•  Applicants who are International Students must not have been admitted to their current school via the MOE AEIS.  
•  Applicants who are on Leave of Absence (LOA) from their current school and are in IP are not eligible to apply.  
  
All applicants are required to sit for a General Ability Test (GAT) in ACS (Independent) on Friday, 16 October 2026 at 2pm (subject to change at school’s discretion).  Please note that taking the GAT is compulsory and there is no alternative date.  **A test fee of $76.30 per applicant will be levied**.
