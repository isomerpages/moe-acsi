<p><strong>JAE (Joint Admission Exercise)</strong>&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>The 2026 JAE posting results will be released on Tuesday,&nbsp;3&nbsp;February
2026.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>All posted students are to report to school on 4&nbsp;February 2026. Please
report at 7:45 am outside the ACS(I)&nbsp;Administration Office&nbsp;in
your secondary school uniform. Further information on the orientation programme
and other school-related matters will be shared with you at that time.&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>2026&nbsp;JAE (Joint Admission Exercise) Appeal</strong>&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>Students who wish to appeal for admission into 2026&nbsp;IBDP after the
release of the Joint Admission Exercise (JAE) posting may do so from 8:00
am on Tuesday, 03 February 2026 to 12:00 pm on Monday, 9 February 2026.
&nbsp;For more information on appeals, please visit <a rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>https://www.acsindep.moe.edu.sg/admissions/joint-admissions-exercise/appeals/</u></a>.</p>
