<h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Direct School Admission into ACS(I) (Year 5)</strong></h4>

<p>Direct School Admission-Junior College (DSA-JC) provides a pathway for students to gain direct entry into junior colleges based on their diverse talents and achievements, beyond their performance at the Singapore-Cambridge GCE O-Level Examination.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Students successfully allocated a school via DSA-JC will <u>not</u> be allowed to participate in the JAE as places have already been reserved for them in the DSA-JC schools.&nbsp; They will also not be allowed to transfer to another JC after the release of the GCE O-Level examination results.&nbsp; They are expected to honour their commitment to the DSA-JC schools.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Students may apply through DSA-JC to Anglo-Chinese School (Independent) for the listed specific talent areas.&nbsp; The final selection is dependent on various factors such as the number and quality of applications received, the school’s talent requirements, the availability of suitable candidates and vacancies.&nbsp; There is no guarantee that at least one offer will be granted for every listed talent area as selection is dependent on these factors.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Applicants who want to participate in Anglo-Chinese School (Independent)’s DSA-JC exercise can apply through ACS(I) DSA-JC Portal under <strong>ONE talent domain only.</strong>&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
