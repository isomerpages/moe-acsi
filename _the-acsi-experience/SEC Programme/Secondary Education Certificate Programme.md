<p style="text-align: justify;">The ACS (Independent) Secondary Education Certificate is a four-year course leading to the Secondary Education Certificate (SEC) examinations. Students may use their SEC results to apply for admission to the ‘A’ levels or the International Baccalaureate Diploma Programme (IBDP) and polytechnics in Singapore or overseas.</p>
