<p style="text-align: justify;">The ACS (Independent) Secondary Education Certificate Programme is a four-year course leading to the Secondary Education Certificate examinations. Students may use their SEC results to apply for admission to the ‘A’ levels or International Baccalaureate Diploma Programme (IBDP) and polytechnics in Singapore or overseas.</p>
