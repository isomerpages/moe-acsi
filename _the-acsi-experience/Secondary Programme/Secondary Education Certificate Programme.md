<p>&lt;p style="text-align: justify;"&gt;The ACS (Independent) Secondary
Education Certificate is a four-year course leading to the Secondary Education
Certificate (SEC) examinations. Students may use their SEC results to apply
for admission to the &amp;lsquo;A&amp;rsquo; levels or the International
Baccalaureate Diploma Programme (IBDP) and polytechnics in Singapore or
overseas.&lt;/p&gt;</p>
