<p>Year 3 SEC and IP students are invited to enrol in one AC Modular Programme
(ACMP) module.</p>
<p>&nbsp;ACMP aims to equip students with future-ready skills and skills
in the ACS(I) Skill-Groups in addition to:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Giving students a flavour of various <strong>Career</strong> pathways.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Encouraging intellectual curiosity with an array of <strong>Academic</strong> modules.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Instilling the importance of shining our light in <strong>Service</strong> to
the community&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>The modules on offer in 2026 are:&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>A</strong>cademic: Advanced Space Physics</p>
<p><strong>C</strong>areer: Financial Literacy, Law, Design Thinking, Data
Analytics with Python, and AI Vision with VEX AIM</p>
<p><strong>S</strong>ervice: Self-initiated VIA or VIA with designated partner
organisation</p>
